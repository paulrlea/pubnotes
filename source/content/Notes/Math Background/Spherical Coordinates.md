$x = r\sin \theta \cos \phi$
$y=r\sin \theta \sin \phi$
$z=r\cos \theta$

Where the $\phi$ is known as the *azimuthal* angle, $\theta$ is the *polar* angle and $r$ is the radius. These two angles are shown below:
